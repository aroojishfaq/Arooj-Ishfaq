HARRY POTTER SERIES BY J.K.ROWLING

Basic Theme:

The novels chronicle the lives of a young wizard, Harry Potter, and his friends Hermione Granger and Ron Weasley, all of whom are students at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. The main story arc concerns Harry's struggle against Lord Voldemort, a dark wizard who intends to become immortal, overthrow the wizard governing body known as the Ministry of Magic and subjugate all wizards and Muggles (non-magical people). There are different series of Harry Potter regarding the tales of his different adventures. Some of them are in chronological order and some are just in throwback seasons.The movies are also made on Harry Potter based on its novel casting Daniel Radcliffe as Harry Potter, Emma Watson as Hermione Granger and Rupert Grint as Ron Weasley.
